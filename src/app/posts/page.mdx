## Sealed unit survey app

As its easy to forget a detail when on the go I created this survey app
for double or triple glazed units.

![Survey app image](/images/surveyApp.png "Survey App")

I made good use of Css Anchor for pop up information and Html dialog for modals to pop up giving validation of deletion to ensure you can't accidently remove important information. Sharing with Sms, WhatsApp and saving to Pdf has worked great.

Using Solid Start instead of React or Next Js was a new experience. It was nice not having to worry about hydration and making sure to have useEffect to keep the page updated when needed. It uses signals instead of use state which has its differences too.

I have already used once on the job with success, and will continue to do so while I apply updates to gradually improve and tweak where necessary.
